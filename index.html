The world is divided into Gyms, each specializing in a unique set of moves. Prove your skill to unlock them.

Gym Leaders know all the moves—defeat them to earn a medal and move one step closer to the championship.

Battle System

Before entering a gym battle, use our dedicated system to validate your move set and confirm your eligibility. If you meet the criteria, you can challenge the Gym Leader in a real-time dance battle.
