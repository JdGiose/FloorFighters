HOW IT WORKS

Collect Moves • Build Skill • Challenge the Gym • Earn Your Rank

1. Start Your Journey (£1)

Download the B-Boy Battle Guide and unlock the full system. Learn all 90+ moves, follow structured training, and understand each gym.

Get The Guide

2. Build Your Move Set

Train. Practice. Record your progress. Upload photos/videos of your moves and build your personal profile.

Each move brings you closer to qualifying for a gym battle.

3. Prove You’re Ready

Once you have enough moves — screenshot your profile and send your evidence.

If approved, you’ll be invited to challenge a gym leader.

4. Get Invited to Battle (£50)

Receive available locations and dates. Choose your gym. Lock it in.

You now have 2 weeks to prepare.

5. Show Up. Battle. Win.

Step into the arena. Face the gym leader. This is real. No shortcuts.

Win the battle → earn your medal, certification, and official record.

6. Become a Gym Leader

Train consistently through the full program (24–52 weeks). Earn your reputation. Prove your level.

  • Judge fighters
  • Earn £10 per battle

This is not given. This is earned.
